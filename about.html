About

 

The Indian Knowledge Systems Research Institute (IKSRI) stands at the forefront of blending ancient Indian wisdom with contemporary scientific research to address today�s global challenges. With a vision to be a leading global center in the research and application of Indian Knowledge Systems (IKS), IKSRI is dedicated to benefiting humanity through sustainable, evidence-based solutions across healthcare, technology, and environmental conservation.

 

Innovative Research and Sustainable Solutions

At IKSRI, we are committed to academic integrity, cultural sensitivity, and interdisciplinary collaboration, ensuring that our research not only respects traditional knowledge but also meets modern scientific standards. Our areas of focus span from rigorous literature research and scientific model development to clinical trials and cutting-edge technology development, all aimed at harnessing the power of IKS for sustainable development and social impact.

 

A Collaborative Approach to Bridging Traditions and Science

Our organizational structure supports a collaborative environment, with research departments, a clinical research unit, and a technology development unit working together to translate ancient wisdom into practical, innovative solutions. Our dedication to sustainability is mirrored in our funding strategies and our efforts to disseminate knowledge through publications, media collaboration, and educational initiatives.

 

Join Us in Shaping a Sustainable Future

IKSRI invites researchers, practitioners, and the public to explore the richness of Indian Knowledge Systems and join us in our mission to integrate this ancient wisdom with modern practices for a healthier, more sustainable world. Discover the potential of IKS to address contemporary challenges and contribute to the global good with IKSRI.
